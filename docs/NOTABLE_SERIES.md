# Notable Recurring Event Series

Documented recurring J-culture event series in Indonesia.

---

## Nihongo Nihon Bunkasai — Unpad, Bandung
**1973 –**

The earliest documented organized J-culture event in Indonesia. Held at Fakultas Sastra Universitas Padjadjaran (Unpad), Jl. Dipati Ukur, Bandung. Widely considered the origin point of the Bunkasai Unpad tradition that continues today.

- 1973-05-23 — Nihongo Nihon Bunkasai (earliest record)

## Yamato Damashii — STBA YAPARI-ABA, Bandung
**2006 – 2025 | Editions I–XVIII**

Annual J-culture festival organized by HIMADE (Himpunan Mahasiswa Bahasa Jepang) STBA YAPARI-ABA, held at Kampus STBA YAPARI-ABA, Jl. Cihampelas No.194, Bandung.

The series was preceded by **Nihon no Tanoshimimashou** (May 2005), a 2-day event that is considered the direct predecessor.

## Gelar Jepang UI — Universitas Indonesia, Depok
**1995 – 2023 | Editions 1–29**

Annual J-culture festival organized by students of the Japanese Literature program at Universitas Indonesia. Held at the UI campus in Depok (later moved to off-campus venues).

## Jak-Japan Matsuri
**2009 – 2022 | Annual**

Annual Japanese cultural festival jointly organized by the Embassy of Japan and Indonesian institutions. Held at Gelora Bung Karno (GBK), Senayan, Jakarta.

---

## Nihon no Matsuri — Bandung
**2007 – present | Annual**

Annual J-culture festival in Bandung. First edition: 2007-12-01.
